TrendyReels collects minimal information to provide our service:

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Cloudflare: Content delivery network and hosting provider.

4. Data Retention

We retain video metadata and site settings indefinitely to maintain the platform. No personal user data is stored as we do not require user accounts.
